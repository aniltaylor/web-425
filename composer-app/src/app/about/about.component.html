Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart

Joannes Chrysostomus Wolfgangus Theophilus Mozart, was a prolific and influential composer of the Classical period. Mozart performed as a pianist, in a competition before the Emperor with Muzaio Clementi on December 24th of 1781. He wrote the opera "The Abduction from the Seraglio" among others. His most well-known works are "serenade No. 13", "Symphony No. 41", and "the magic flute." Mozart was a grand composer and his works are still being used today in many applications. Source
